# Product Podcasts

A curated list of podcasts for product managers — in Portuguese and English. Organized by language, with a short note on what each one covers.

---

## Portuguese

**Product Guru's**
One of the most established Brazilian PM podcasts. Covers the full spectrum — strategy, metrics, career, and technical product management. Episode #99 with Vagner Fiuza on APIs is particularly good for non-technical PMs.
[Listen on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/7uRmxP1l3m9kwrsTYxnIMA)

**Produto pelo Mundo**
Interviews with Brazilian PMs working internationally. Useful if you're navigating a career transition to Europe or the US.
[Listen on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/5PD1bRaJz3irxq712zF566)

**Mulheres de Produto**
Focused on women in product. Covers leadership, career development, and the structural challenges of the profession.
[Listen on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/1rfUWxL2Ia7kA343ClLya1)

**Analytics Talk**
Data and analytics focused, with guests from product and data science backgrounds. Strong for anyone looking to deepen their quantitative skills.
[Listen on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/4qP97l3OJO9vH37fWhrbB7)

---

## English

**This Is Product Management**
Interviews with PMs from a wide range of industries. Episodes vary significantly in depth — worth filtering by topic rather than listening chronologically.
[Listen on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/5qoM5evv8FUvaqkSY6OHzn)

**The Product Podcast**
Produced by Product School. Practical content on PM skills, career, and frameworks. Good for structured learning.
[Listen on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/1XBrhVLsQOIAv3KFBqnzrX)

**Metric Stack**
Analytics and data-focused. Covers how product and data teams collaborate, measurement strategy, and experimentation. Recommended for anyone working in growth or analytics.
[Listen on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/7ksflmEcFHX9XQOlMlE25Z)

**MetrikUp**
Focused on product metrics and growth. More technical than most PM podcasts — good for PMs who want to go deeper on measurement.
[Listen on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/2p60u4FF6HIrNX5CDQbF69)
